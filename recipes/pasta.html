Pasta

Description

Pasta is a type of Italian food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: Penne, spaghetti, fettuccine, etc.
  • Olive oil: For tossing cooked pasta.
  • Salt: To season pasta water.
  • Sauce: Marinara, alfredo, pesto, etc.
  • Herbs: Basil, oregano, parsley.
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta.
  • Protein: Chicken, shrimp, meatballs.
  • Vegetables: Spinach, broccoli, tomatoes.

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook until al dente.
  3. Drain pasta, reserving some pasta water.
  4. Toss pasta with sauce and any other ingredients.
  5. Add pasta water to adjust consistency if needed.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with herbs and cheese.
